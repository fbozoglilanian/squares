Welcome to Squares! you can play the game on https://squaresgame.herokuapp.com/

The objective is really simple, you need to paint a wall using squares. For each square you paint you have a point but if you paint groups of 4 you earn more points. Make as many squares as you can and compete with your family and friends.

How to play?

  1. Create or join a room by name.
  2. Invite other friends by sending the page url.
  3. Create as many groups of 4 squares as possible until completing the wall.
